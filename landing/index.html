Built-In Financial Metrics

Every metric a real estate investor needs, calculated automatically from your data.

Cap Rate
Annual NOI ÷ market value — yield on the asset
DSCR
Monthly NOI ÷ monthly mortgage — can income service the debt?
ICR
Annual NOI ÷ annual interest — interest coverage ratio
OER
Operating expenses ÷ gross income — cost efficiency
Cash-on-Cash
Annual cash flow ÷ equity — return on invested capital
IRR
Internal rate of return — annualised return across all cash flows
Economic Vacancy
Lost rent from vacancy ÷ potential rent — event-based calculation
Payback Period
Time for cumulative cash flow to recover total expenses
